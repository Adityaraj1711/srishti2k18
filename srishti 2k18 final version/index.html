About SRISHTI 2K18.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION.

Q.What is SRISHTI 2K18?

SRISHTI 2K18 is the technical symposium organized by LNCT Bhopal instituted for the enrichment of Engineering and Science. Started with the aim of providing a platform for the Indian student community to assemble, ideate, interact, and share knowledge and develop and showcase their technical prowers.

Q.What does SRISHTI 2K18 include?

SRISHTI 2K18 includes the following –

  • Innovative events to test the technical and general knowledge of the participant.
  • Intuitive workshops to nurture the abilities of the participant.
  • Inceptive initiatives to make society a better place to live in.
  • Incredible shows to illustrate and enjoy the beauty of technology.

Q.When and where is SRISHTI 2K18 going to be held?

SRISHTI 2k18 would be a grandiose 3-day festival to be held from 1st NOV to 3rd NOV 2018 in the campus of LNCT Bhopal.

Q.How will a participant get benefited by attending SRISHTI 2K18?

  • SRISHTI 2k18 is a platform to test the abilities of a participant when exposed to a physical problem; this makes the participant aware of his strengths and weaknesses.
  • SRISHTI 2k18 is a platform for the participants to interact with the industry making them aware of the practical problem faced and the corresponding solution.
  • SRISHTI 2k18 is a platform to bring out the innovative child hidden in every student; this refreshes the participant and gives him/her memories to cherish throughout his/her life.

Q.Who can participate in SRISHTI 2K18?

A.Any student (all over INDIA) having a valid student ID card of their college or university will be entertained in the fest.

Q.When will the registration open?

A.Registrations are open. Get your tickets ASAP.

Q.Do we get any certificate for participation?

A.Yes, participation certificates will be given to all participants.

Q.I don't find my question listed here. What can I do?

A.You can always contact us at srishtiecofest@lnct.ac.in. We would be more than happy to help you.
